The Earth's Fever

Wren stood on her porch, watching the heat waves shimmer above the brown grass.

"The earth feels like it has a fever," she whispered.

She looked across the street at the forest. It seemed to hum with life, cool and green.

"The forest knows the secrets. I need to explore the neighborhood and learn how to cool things down."

But here's the challenge: You have limited time and resources. Every decision matters. Some choices will help, others might backfire. You must balance the temperature, biodiversity, and community support.

⚠️ This won't be easy. The Earth's fever is spreading fast.
